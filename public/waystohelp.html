WAYS TO HELP
Ways to Help

As an all-volunteer organization, it is only with your generous support that Operation: Children continues to sponsor the Adoption Division of the Los Angeles County Department of Children and Family Services. Your donations directly help prospective parents have the opportunity to meet and interact with hard-to-place older and multiple-sibling children in a relaxed and fun setting.

It is rewarding to witness the union of a homeless child with wanting parents. Even more rewarding is see hard to place children – as many as 7 siblings – placed with one set of adoptive parents.

Become A Member
One of the best ways to help is to volunteer your time and talents. As an all-volunteer organization, member participation is essential. No monies are spent on staffing. 100% of all monies raised go directly to benefit the children.

Planned Giving
Consider making a regular monthly donation to Operation: Children. This is an excellent way to help foster children find permanent homes on an on-going basis throughout the year. Reminders can be sent to you by email or regular mail.

Gift To Operation: Children
You may also Gift to Operation: Children in your will, estate or in memory of a friend or loved one. We will mail a confirmation of your donation to the recipient you designate. Please contact Deborah Greene at Deborah@OperationChildren-la.org for more information.
