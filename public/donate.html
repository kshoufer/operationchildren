Donate

Operation: Children is able to help hundreds of children in the Los Angeles Country Foster Care system find a permanent home to call their own, thanks to the support of generous, individual donors.

Your donation enables more children to grow up with someone to call "Mom" or "Dad", and have the security of knowing that they won't be moved again.

We welcome all levels of donation, whether it's a one-time gift, or a monthly donation. You may choose to make a donation in memory of a friend or loved one. We will mail a confirmation of your donation to the recipient you designate.
