 ABOUT
Our Mission: To raise funds to help better the lives of disadvantaged children within Los Angeles County.

Operation: Children incorporated in 1984 with the purpose of raising funds for children in need within Los Angeles County. Since we are an all-volunteer organization, every dollar donated goes directly to better the lives of children in Los Angeles County.

Operation: Children presently supports the Los Angeles County Department of Children and Family Services (DCFS) co-sponsoring and assisting in the PROJECT ADOPT programs. Operation: Children financially supports the County's "Adoption Festivals" and helps with their staffing several times annually.

These children, sometimes housed in residential-care facilities, are usually neglected or abandoned. There are many multiple-sibling children who desperately want to be placed together in a permanent home.

The Adoption Festivals have been extremely successful matching multiple siblings with the same adoptive parents, and in finding homes for older, harder-to-place children. Over one-third of the children who attend an adoption festival are adopted.
