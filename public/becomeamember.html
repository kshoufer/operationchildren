Become a Member

Operation: Children is a 501(c)(3) Non-Profit Charitable Corporation, with an all-volunteer staff. Every dollar donated goes directly to better the lives of children in Los Angeles County.

With the help of our members we make significant contributions for the betterment of the lives of hundreds of children each year.

We continually seek new members and supporters to help us realize our goal of finding a permanent family for hard-to-place children in foster care in Los Angeles County.
