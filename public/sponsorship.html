Sponsorship

As well as donating outright or becoming a member, various individuals and businesses contribute their services or merchandise as needed in support of one or more of our events — centerpieces, space, beverages, food or just time. Contributions can be valuable PR and advertising, as well as a possible tax deduction (please check with your tax advisor).

Help underwrite any one of a number of specific items for our annual fundraiser, sponsor an event, a service or donate an item for auction. All forms of sponsorship will receive recognition on our website, or remain anonymous if you prefer. You may choose to make a donation in memory of a friend or loved one. We will mail a confirmation to the recipient you designate.
Please contact Deborah@OperationChildren-la.org for more information
