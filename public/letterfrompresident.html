Message from the President
July, 2014

Happy Summer everyone! All of us here at Operation:Children hope you are all having a safe and enjoyable summer season. At Operation:Children, we continue our ongoing mission to help find permanent, loving homes for those children in the Los Angeles County Foster Care System needing a home of their own, with a forever family, to spend their summers, falls, winters and springs!

Operation:Children has been busy! So far this year we’ve sponsored Seven (7) Adoption and/or Matching Events held by the Los Angeles County Department of Children and Family Services Adoption Division and/or Kidsave’s Weekend Miracles Program. Every time we can get Foster Children and Applicant Families together in a relaxed setting doing an activity together – it increases a child’s chance of finding a permanent home by 33%. These events let the children’s personalities shine, and relaxes the families too!

Operation:Children provides the much needed funds, and our members volunteer to help staff these important events that match up children wanting to be adopted with parents looking to adopt and/or host children. To continue our work, we must fundraise. So we do it again, on October 18, 2014 at the Bel Air Country Club! An evening not to be missed, as we’ll be having a black-tie optional event, complete with dinner, dancing and casino action! Please help support us in whatever area you can – be it by donating an auction item, becoming a sponsor, or attending the event – all this help us achieve our goal, and it’s fun too! Please click here for sponsorship, donation and ticket information.

I wish to once again thank all our Operation:Children members, friends and supporters for their ongoing commitment to this organization. It is with this outstanding support that we continue to provide much needed funding for the Los Angeles County Adoption Division of the Department of Children and Family Services (DCFS) PROJECT ADOPT programs, and in partnership with Kidsave, who works with the children ages 9 to 18 seeking a permanent home and/or adult connection before aging out of the system.

Our next General Membership meeting date is August 20, 2014, and will be an evening meeting. Please email our Membership Chair, Karen Romero, at davidr1907@roadrunner.com, or myself at Lholcombe7@gmail.com, or phone (310) 963-8506, for more information about becoming a member or attending a meeting.

With warmest wishes,
Linda Holcombe
President 2014
