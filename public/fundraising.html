Fundraising

Annual Fundraiser
We are in the very early stages of planning our next fundraiser. Do you have a talent or ideas for us? Please share! As with any all-volunteer organization, we are always in need of fresh ideas, or new twists on old ones!!

Our annual fundraisers help us to support the Los Angeles County of Children and Family Services Adoption Department, and Kidsave, to help find loving, forever families for hard-to-place children in need of permanent homes.

Our last annual fundraiser was held at the magnificent Petersen Automotive Museum. "Driving the Dream" and featured an evening of dining and dancing while surrounded by some of the world's most stunning and notable automobiles. Live and silent auctions added fun for all. Our annual fundraisers help us to support the Los Angeles County of Children and Family Services and Kidsave to help find loving parents for the hard-to-place children in foster care needing permanent homes.

For more information on sponsorship and underwriting opportunities please contact linda@operationchildren-la.org.

Holiday parties
Operation: Children hosts a handful of holiday parties throughout the year for our members and prospective members at private homes. These parties raise funds with the price of the ticket, and sometimes a small auction. It's a fun way for our members to have some social time together, and help the children too!
